How to Choose the Right Floor Scrubber for Large Manufacturing Plants. 如何为大型制造工厂选择最合适的洗地机？

March 15, 20262026年3月15日 · 8 min read阅读约8分钟 · ELVONIS Engineering TeamELVONIS 工程团队

Key Takeaway: The right floor scrubber depends on your facility size, floor type, soiling level, and whether you need battery or mains power. This guide walks you through every variable. 核心要点：选择洗地机取决于场地面积、地面材质、污染程度以及是否需要电池或市电驱动。本指南将逐一解析每个关键变量。

1. Understand Your Floor Area 1. 明确您的场地面积

The single most important factor in selecting a floor scrubber is your total floor area. Facilities under 5,000 sq ft benefit from walk-behind models. Above 20,000 sq ft, a ride-on scrubber delivers dramatically better productivity — cleaning up to 20,000 sq ft per hour versus 8,000 sq ft for walk-behind models. 选择洗地机最重要的因素是总场地面积。5,000平方尺以下的场所适合步行式机型；超过20,000平方尺的场所，驾驶式洗地机的效率将大幅提升——每小时可清洁高达20,000平方尺，而步行式仅约8,000平方尺。

2. Match the Brush to Your Floor Type 2. 根据地面材质选择刷型

Disc brushes are ideal for flat epoxy or sealed concrete floors. Cylindrical brush systems excel on uneven, grouted, or heavily contaminated surfaces, offering side-sweeping action that disc models cannot replicate. For manufacturing plants with metalworking swarf or heavy oils, a cylindrical system with a robust suction motor is essential. 圆盘刷适合平整的环氧地坪或密封混凝土地面；圆柱刷系统则在不平整、有接缝或重度污染的地面表现优异，其侧扫功能是圆盘机型无法替代的。对于有金属切屑或重度油污的制造工厂，配备强力吸污电机的圆柱刷系统是必选项。

3. Battery vs Mains Power 3. 电池驱动 vs 市电驱动

LFP (lithium iron phosphate) battery models offer 4–8 hours of runtime with opportunity charging during breaks — ideal for large facilities. Mains-powered models are better suited to fixed cleaning stations or facilities with reliable power access throughout the floor. Consider runtime, charging infrastructure, and total cost of ownership over a 5-year horizon. 磷酸铁锂（LFP）电池机型可提供4-8小时续航，并支持间歇充电，适合大型场所；市电机型则更适合固定清洁站或全场地供电稳定的场所。选型时需综合考量续航时间、充电基础设施及5年全生命周期总成本。
