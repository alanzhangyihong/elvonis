    Why Equipment Supply Fails — For Both Users and Distributors 为什么设备供应会失败——对用户和经销商都是如此

    End users face downtime. Distributors face lost customers. The root cause is the same: lack of control over what goes into the machine. 最终用户面临停机，经销商面临客户流失。根源是同一个：对机器内部配置缺乏控制。

    The Missing Layer: Engineering Control 缺失的层次：工程控制

    Factories manufacture. ELVONIS defines specifications, selects components, and ensures consistency before production begins. 工厂负责制造。ELVONIS负责定义规格、选择零部件，并在生产开始前确保一致性。

    Zero Fragile Electronics拒绝脆弱电子件

    Electronics → Failure Risk电子件 → 故障风险

    Mechanical relay architecture eliminates circuit board failure in high-humidity environments. IP54-sealed. No PCB to corrode.纯机械继电器架构消除高湿度环境下的电路板腐蚀故障。IP54密封，无裸露电路。

    Serviceable-First Design易维修优先设计

    Parts → Replacement Speed配件 → 替换速度

    Globally standardized components — not proprietary parts. Source locally, fix fast.全球标准通用部件，非专属锁定配件。就地采购，快速修复。
