    The Real Problem真正的问题

    Why Floor Treatment Often Fails为什么地面处理经常失败

    Uneven pressure, unstable rotation, and wrong pad selection lead to inconsistent results and costly surface damage.压力不均、转速不稳、垫片选错——导致效果不一致和代价高昂的地面损伤。

    ✕ Unstable Rotation✕ 转速不稳

    Belt-driven machines lose torque under load.皮带传动机器在负载下扭矩衰减。

    ✕ Surface Damage✕ 地面损伤

    Wrong pad + uncontrolled speed = burn marks and irreversible damage.错误垫片+失控转速=烧痕与不可逆损伤。

    Speed Is Not the Solution转速不是解决方案

    Higher RPM does not mean better results. What matters is controlled torque — not maximum speed.更高的RPM不等于更好的结果。真正重要的是受控扭矩——而非最高转速。
