**One janitor costs over $35,000 a year when you factor in everything.**

A commercial floor scrubber costs $3,000 — and lasts 8-10 years.

Within 18 months, the machine has already paid for itself.

Don't believe it? Let's run the numbers.

 

## **The True Cost of Manual Cleaning**

Most managers only see the base wage. The real cost is much higher.

**Direct costs:**

Wages: $2,500 – $3,500 / month per person

Benefits, insurance, taxes: ~$600 / month per person

Annual bonus + overtime: $1,500 – $3,000 / year per person

**Hidden costs:**

Cleaning supplies (mops, chemicals, buckets): $500 – $800 / year per person

Hiring & training turnover: ~$800 per hire (janitorial annual turnover rate exceeds 40%)

Management time: 30-60 minutes per day supervising cleaning staff

**Total: One janitorial employee costs $35,000 – $50,000 per year**

## **The True Cost of a Commercial Floor Scrubber**

**Equipment cost:**

Mid-size walk-behind scrubber: $3,000 – $6,000 (8-10 year lifespan)

Annualized equipment cost: $300 – $750 / year

**Operating costs:**

Electricity: $80 – $150 / year (1-2 hrs daily operation)

Consumables (brush pads, squeegee blades): $200 – $400 / year

Maintenance: $150 – $300 / year

**Total: Floor scrubber costs approximately $700 – $1,600 / year**

## **10-Year Cost Comparison**

Example: 3,000㎡ shopping mall requiring 2 janitors OR 1 floor scrubber:

Manual: 2 people × $40,000/yr × 10 years = $800,000

Scrubber: $5,000 (equipment) + $1,200/yr × 10 years = $17,000

**10-year savings: over $780,000**

Even accounting for equipment replacement cycles, the scrubber costs less than 3% of manual labor over a decade.

## **Best Use Cases for Replacing Manual Cleaning**

**Factories & Warehouses**

Large area, heavy soiling, high frequency — maximum ROI for floor scrubbers.

One ride-on scrubber replaces 3-5 janitors' workload.

**Shopping Malls & Supermarkets**

Clean before opening. One machine in 2 hours does what 5 people do in half a day.

**Hotel Chains**

Standardized cleaning across every location. Save 1-2 staff per property.

Scale the ROI across multiple locations.

**Hospitals & Care Facilities**

Consistent cleaning standards regardless of staff turnover.

**Airports, Train Stations & Subway Systems**

Machines don't fatigue, call in sick, or demand raises.

## **When Is a Floor Scrubber NOT Worth It?**

Areas under 300㎡ — manual cleaning is more flexible and cost-effective.

Heavily obstructed floors — reduces machine efficiency significantly.

Very low frequency (less than 2x/week) — payback period extends beyond 5 years.

## **How to Maximize Your Floor Scrubber ROI**

Create a daily cleaning schedule to ensure consistent machine utilization.

Perform regular maintenance: clean brush pads, replace squeegee blades on schedule.

Train operators properly to prevent misuse damage.

Choose a brand with local service support to minimize downtime.

## **Final Thoughts**

The numbers don't lie.

A commercial floor scrubber isn't an expense — it's an investment that delivers returns for a decade.

## **一、人工清洁的真实成本有多高？**

很多管理者只看到了保洁员的工资，却忽略了隐性成本。

**显性成本：**

月薪：3,500 - 5,000元 / 人

社保公积金：约1,200元 / 人 / 月

年终奖 + 节假日加班费：约5,000 - 8,000元 / 人 / 年

**隐性成本：**

清洁耗材（拖把、清洁剂、水桶）：约2,000 - 3,000元 / 人 / 年

人员流动招聘培训成本：约1,500元 / 次（保洁行业年流失率高达40%）

管理时间成本：主管每天花在保洁管理上的时间约30-60分钟

**合计：一名保洁员每年真实成本约 60,000 - 80,000元**

## **二、商用洗地机的真实成本有多低？**

**设备采购成本：**

中型手推洗地机：约15,000 - 25,000元（使用寿命8-10年）

折合每年设备成本：约1,500 - 3,000元

**运营成本：**

电费：约300 - 600元 / 年（每天1-2小时作业）

耗材（刷盘、刮水条）：约800 - 1,500元 / 年

维护保养：约500 - 1,000元 / 年

**合计：洗地机每年总成本约 3,000 - 6,000元**

## **三、10年成本对比一览表**

以一个3,000㎡的商场为例（需要2名保洁员 OR 1台洗地机）：

人工方案：2人 × 70,000元 × 10年 = 1,400,000元

洗地机方案：25,000（设备）+ 5,000元/年 × 10年 = 75,000元

**10年节省：超过 1,300,000元**

即便考虑设备更换周期，洗地机方案的总成本不足人工方案的十分之一。

## **四、哪些场景最适合用洗地机替代人工？**

**工厂车间 / 仓库**

面积大、污染重、清洁频率高——洗地机最能发挥优势。

单台驾驶式洗地机可替代3-5名保洁员的工作量。

**大型商场 / 超市**

营业时间外快速清洁，早晨开门前地面光洁如镜。

一台洗地机2小时完成人工需要5人干半天的活。

**连锁酒店 / 宾馆**

标准化清洁流程，每家分店节省1-2名保洁员人力。

多店复用，投资回报率成倍放大。

**医院 / 养老机构**

稳定的清洁质量，减少因人员变动导致的清洁标准波动。

**机场 / 高铁站 / 地铁站**

全天候高频清洁需求，机器不疲倦、不请假、不涨薪。

## **五、洗地机什么时候不划算？**

面积小于300㎡的场地，人工灵活度更高，买机器可能得不偿失。

地面凹凸不平或障碍物过多的场地，洗地机效率会大幅下降。

清洁频率极低（每周少于2次）的场所，ROI回收期会延长到5年以上。

## **六、如何最大化洗地机的投资回报？**

制定每日清洁计划，保证设备利用率。

按时保养，延长设备寿命（清洗刷盘、更换刮水条）。

培训操作员正确使用，避免误操作损坏设备。

选择有本地售后的品牌，减少停机损失。
