  **机械制造厂**

  金属切屑、铁粉、冷却液喷溅——工业干湿两用吸尘器一机搞定。

  可搭配机床直连使用，边加工边吸尘，保持车间整洁。

  **① 干吸还是干湿两用？**

  普通粉尘：布袋过滤即可。细粉尘/有害粉尘：选HEPA H13/H14级滤芯。

  **④ 容积大小**

  作业量小：20-40L桶容量。中型车间：60-100L。大型工厂：100L以上或中央吸尘系统。

A regular vacuum won't survive a week on a factory floor.

Industrial dust, metal shavings, and hazardous particles demand a completely different class of machine.

Use the wrong vacuum cleaner and you risk equipment failure — or worse, a dust explosion.

Here's how to choose the right industrial vacuum cleaner.

 

## **Industrial Vacuum vs. Consumer Vacuum: What's the Difference?**

Industrial vacuums are engineered for demanding environments:

Suction power exceeds 25kPa. Tank capacity ranges from 40L to 200L. Filtration efficiency reaches 99.9%.

Designed for continuous 8-12 hour operation without overheating or clogging.

## **Where Are Industrial Vacuums Used?**

**Metal Fabrication & Machine Shops**

Metal shavings, iron powder, and coolant spray require wet/dry industrial vacuums.

Direct machine connection keeps work areas clean during operation.

**Construction Sites**

Cement dust, sand, and plaster powder create dangerous PM2.5 levels.

HEPA-equipped industrial vacuums protect workers and comply with safety standards.

**Food Processing Plants**

Flour and sugar dust is highly combustible.

Explosion-proof certified vacuums are mandatory to prevent ignition risks.

**Hospitals & Clean Rooms**

Medical-grade HEPA vacuums achieve 0.3-micron filtration at 99.97% capture rate.

Essential in operating rooms, labs, and sterile environments.

**Tunnels & Underground Projects**

Extreme dust volumes require high-capacity mobile units.

Three-phase industrial vacuums deliver the power needed for heavy construction.

**Glass & Ceramics Manufacturing**

Silica dust causes irreversible lung disease.

Industrial vacuums with fine filtration are a legal and moral requirement.

## **How to Choose the Right Industrial Vacuum**

**① Dry-Only or Wet/Dry?**

Dry dust only: standard dry vacuum. Liquids involved: choose wet/dry combo.

**② Do You Need Explosion-Proof Certification?**

Food plants, chemical facilities, and flour mills must use ATEX-certified models.

**③ Filtration Level**

General dust: standard bag filter works. Fine or toxic dust: HEPA H13/H14 required.

**④ Tank Size**

Light use: 20-40L. Medium workshops: 60-100L. Large plants: 100L+ or central vacuum system.

## **Industrial Vacuum Price Ranges**

Small industrial vacuum (20-40L): $150 – $600

Mid-size industrial vacuum (60-80L): $500 – $1,500

Explosion-proof industrial vacuum: $2,500 – $10,000

Central vacuum system: from $8,000+

## **Maintenance Tips**

Clean or replace filters every 100 operating hours.

Never exceed liquid capacity — it damages the motor.

Empty the dust bin after every use to prevent secondary contamination.

## **Final Thoughts**

The right industrial vacuum protects your workers, your equipment, and your facility.

The wrong one creates risk — and costs more in the long run.
