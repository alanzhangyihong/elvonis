A mop can't do what a floor scrubber can.

One machine. 10 minutes. Cleaner floors than 2 hours of manual mopping.

But with so many models — ride-on, walk-behind, scrubber-sweeper combos — how do you choose?

This guide breaks it all down.

## **What Is a Commercial Floor Scrubber?**

A commercial floor scrubber simultaneously dispenses water, scrubs the surface, and vacuums up dirty water — in one single pass.

Compared to traditional mopping, it's 3-5x faster and reduces labor costs by over 60%.

## **Where Are Commercial Floor Scrubbers Used?**

**Shopping Malls & Supermarkets**

High foot traffic means dirt accumulates fast.

Daily scrubbing keeps floors spotless and improves the shopping experience.

**Hospitals & Clinics**

Hygiene standards in healthcare facilities are non-negotiable.

With disinfectant solution, a floor scrubber effectively reduces cross-contamination risk.

**Factories & Warehouses**

Oil residue, metal shavings, and fine dust require industrial-grade cleaning.

Heavy-duty scrubbers with high brush pressure tackle the toughest floors.

**Hotels & Hospitality**

Lobbies and corridors need cleaning multiple times per day.

Quiet, efficient scrubbers clean without disturbing guests.

**Restaurants & Food Processing Plants**

Grease-coated kitchen floors need deep cleaning every single day.

Explosion-proof models are available for sensitive environments.

**Schools, Airports & Exhibition Halls**

Large surface areas make manual cleaning impractical and expensive.

Ride-on scrubbers can cover thousands of square meters in a single session.

## **Key Specs to Evaluate**

**① Cleaning Width (cm/inches)**

Wider = more area per pass. Choose based on your space size.

Under 500㎡: 45-55cm. 500-2,000㎡: 65-80cm. 2,000㎡+: ride-on model.

**② Tank Capacity (liters)**

Larger tanks mean longer run time. Walk-behind: 15-45L. Ride-on: 80-200L.

**③ Brush Pressure (kg)**

Higher pressure removes tough, embedded dirt. For factories, aim for ≥30kg.

**④ Noise Level (dB)**

For hospitals and hotels, opt for machines rated ≤65dB.

## **Top Commercial Floor Scrubber Brands**

Leading brands include Tennant, Kärcher, Comac, and Nilfisk.

Always request a demo. Compare local after-sales support before buying.

## **How Much Does a Commercial Floor Scrubber Cost?**

Walk-behind battery scrubber: $800 – $4,000

Ride-on scrubber: $8,000 – $25,000

Scrubber-sweeper combo: $3,000 – $10,000

Most buyers recover costs within 6-18 months through labor savings.

## **Final Thoughts**

Don't buy the most expensive machine. Buy the right machine for your space.

Know your floor area, surface type, and budget — then compare quotes from 3-5 vendors.

## **三、商用洗地机核心参数怎么看？**

  **① 清洁宽度（cm）**

  清洁宽度越大，单次覆盖面积越广，效率越高。

  建议：500㎡以下选45-55cm；500-2000㎡选65-80cm；2000㎡以上选驾驶式。

  **② 水箱容量（L）**

  水箱越大，连续作业时间越长。一般手推式15-45L，驾驶式80-200L。

  **③ 刷盘压力（kg）**

  压力越大，清洁重污染地面越有效。工厂场景建议选压力≥30kg的机型。

  **④ 噪音（dB）**

  酒店、医院等场所建议选低噪音型号，一般≤65dB为优。

  选品牌时重点看：售后服务网络是否覆盖本地、配件是否易采购、是否提供试机服务。
