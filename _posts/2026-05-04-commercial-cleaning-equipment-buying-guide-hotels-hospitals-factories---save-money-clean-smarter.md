# **Commercial Cleaning Equipment Buying Guide: Hotels, Hospitals & Factories — Save Money, Clean Smarter**

Many businesses buy cleaning equipment that sits unused after three months.

The reason is almost always the same: wrong machine for the wrong space.

This guide helps you avoid every common mistake before you spend a dollar.

## **5 Questions to Answer Before You Buy**

**① What is your cleaning area?**

Under 500㎡: walk-behind equipment. 500-5,000㎡: large walk-behind or ride-on. 5,000㎡+: ride-on + robot combination.

**② What type of flooring do you have?**

Tile, marble, concrete, epoxy, anti-slip surfaces — each requires different brush type and pressure settings.

**③ How heavy is the contamination?**

Light (mostly dust): basic commercial grade. Medium (oil + dust): standard commercial. Heavy (grease + chemicals): industrial grade.

**④ Any special environment requirements?**

Explosion-proof (chemical / food plants), medical grade (hospitals), low-noise (hotels / libraries).

**⑤ What is your budget?**

Define total budget and allocation priorities before approaching vendors.

## **Recommended Equipment by Industry**

**Hotels**

Recommended: Floor scrubber (lobby/ballroom) + handheld vacuum (rooms) + quiet cleaning robot (corridors)

Key requirements: low noise, elegant design, fine cleaning quality.

**Hospitals**

Recommended: Medical-grade scrubber + HEPA industrial vacuum + disinfection cleaning robot

Key requirements: sterilization capability, high filtration precision, medical hygiene compliance.

**Factories & Warehouses**

Recommended: Industrial scrubber + explosion-proof vacuum + pressure washer

Key requirements: durability, high suction, heavy contamination performance.

**Shopping Malls & Supermarkets**

Recommended: Ride-on scrubber + commercial sweeping robot + walk-behind (edges)

Key requirements: high efficiency, wide area coverage, low maintenance.

**Food Service & Restaurants**

Recommended: Hot water pressure washer (kitchen) + walk-behind scrubber (dining hall) + industrial vacuum (dry storage)

Key requirements: heavy degreasing, food safety compliance.

**Schools, Museums & Libraries**

Recommended: Commercial cleaning robot + walk-behind scrubber + compact industrial vacuum

Key requirements: quiet operation, smart scheduling, minimal human supervision.

## **5 Costly Mistakes to Avoid**

**Mistake 1: Choosing by price alone**

Budget machines often sacrifice tank size, brush pressure, and battery. They appear cheap but cost more long-term.

**Mistake 2: Ignoring after-sales support**

A breakdown with no local service center and a two-week wait for parts is more expensive than the machine itself.

Before buying: confirm local service availability and parts stock.

**Mistake 3: Overbuying**

A ride-on scrubber in a 500㎡ space is awkward to maneuver and wasteful.

**Mistake 4: Ignoring electrical capacity**

Commercial equipment uses significant power (220V/380V). Verify your facility's electrical capacity before purchasing.

**Mistake 5: Skipping the demo**

Reputable brands offer free on-site demos. Always test before ordering — it's worth more than any spec sheet.

## **How to Calculate ROI on Cleaning Equipment**

Formula: Annual labor cost savings ÷ Equipment cost = Payback period

Example: Equipment costs $12,000. Eliminates 2 part-time janitors at $20,000/year each = $40,000 savings → Paid back in under 4 months.

In practice, most commercial cleaning equipment pays back within 1-2 years.

## **一、采购前必须回答的5个问题**

**① 清洁面积有多大？**

500㎡以下：手推式设备即可。500-5000㎡：驾驶式或大型手推式。5000㎡以上：驾驶式+机器人组合。

**② 地面是什么材质？**

瓷砖、大理石、水泥、环氧地坪、防滑地坪，不同材质对刷盘和压力要求不同。

**③ 污染程度如何？**

轻度（灰尘为主）：基础型设备。中度（油污+灰尘）：标准商用型。重度（油脂+化学品）：工业级设备。

**④ 有没有特殊环境要求？**

防爆要求（化工、食品）、医用级别（医院手术室）、静音要求（酒店、图书馆）。

**⑤ 预算范围是多少？**

明确总预算，分配采购优先级，避免超支。

## **二、不同行业推荐设备组合**

**酒店行业**

推荐配置：商用洗地机（大堂/宴会厅）+ 手持式吸尘器（客房）+ 低噪音扫地机器人（走廊）

核心诉求：低噪音、外观好看、清洁细腻。

**医院行业**

推荐配置：医用级洗地机 + HEPA工业吸尘器 + 消毒扫地机器人

核心诉求：杀菌消毒、过滤精度高、符合医疗卫生标准。

**工厂/仓库**

推荐配置：工业洗地机 + 防爆工业吸尘器 + 高压清洗机

核心诉求：耐用、大吸力、适合重污染环境。

**商场/超市**

推荐配置：驾驶式洗地机 + 商用扫地机器人 + 手推洗地机（边角）

核心诉求：高效率、覆盖大面积、维护简单。

**餐饮行业**

推荐配置：热水高压清洗机（厨房）+ 手推洗地机（大厅）+ 工业吸尘器（干货区）

核心诉求：去油效果强、符合食品安全规范。

**学校/展馆/图书馆**

推荐配置：商用扫地机器人 + 手推洗地机 + 小型工业吸尘器

核心诉求：低噪、智能、减少人工干预。

## **三、采购时的五大避坑指南**

**坑1：只看价格，不看参数**

低价机型往往缩减了水箱、刷盘压力和电池容量，看起来便宜，用起来贵。

**坑2：忽视售后服务**

设备出故障，本地没有维修点，备件采购要等两周——这才是最贵的代价。

购买前确认：品牌本地有售后网点？配件是否现货供应？

**坑3：一步到位买最贵**

500㎡的场地买驾驶式洗地机，转弯困难、操作不便，反而浪费。

**坑4：忽视用电容量**

商用设备用电量大（220V/380V），采购前确认场地电气容量是否满足。

**坑5：没有申请试机**

优质品牌都提供免费试机服务，实地测试后再下单，比任何参数对比都靠谱。

## **四、如何评估采购的ROI（投资回报）？**

计算公式：年节省人力成本 ÷ 设备总价 = 预计回本年限

示例：设备价格80,000元，每年节省保洁人员2人×40,000元=80,000元 → 1年收回成本。

实际上，多数商用清洁设备的投资回报期在1-2年之间。
